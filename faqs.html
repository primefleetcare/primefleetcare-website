Frequently Asked Questions

Everything fleet managers ask.

Straight answers on coverage, what's included, scheduling and pricing. Still not sure? Call Harry on 0449 162 476.

What areas do you cover?

All Perth metro depots plus the Peel region — from Mandurah in the south up to Yanchep in the north.

How does the mobile service work?

We come to your yard with our own rig, power and equipment. You don't move a truck or lift a finger — we work around your operations, including early mornings, evenings and weekends.

Do I need to supply power or water?

Power — no. We're fully self-contained and bring our own. Water — most depots already have it on tap, which we'll use; if your site doesn't, we can bring our own for a small additional charge.

What's included in a standard wash?

A standard wash is a full exterior degrease and soap wash — body, wheels and trailer come up clean and road-ready.

What extras can I add?

Underbody clean, engine bay clean, interior vacuum & wipe, full cab detail, and ceramic sealant application. Mix and match to suit your fleet.

Is there a lock-in contract?

No lock-in, ever. We do recommend a recurring schedule — weekly, fortnightly or monthly — so your fleet always looks road-ready, but it's completely your call.

How often can you come?

As often as you like — weekly, fortnightly or monthly. We work weekends and after-hours to fit around your operation.

How quickly can you book me in?

Same week. Most yards are booked within 2–3 days.

How much does it cost?

It depends on your fleet size, the wash type and how often we come. The best way to find out is the free trial — we'll wash one truck at your yard at no cost, then give you a straight quote.

How do I get started?

Claim your free trial wash — hit "Book Now" or call 0449 162 476. No deposit, no commitment.
