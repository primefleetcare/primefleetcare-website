Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 June 2026

Prime Fleet Care ("we", "us", "our") provides mobile truck washing and fleet detailing services across Perth, WA. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect your personal information.

What we collect

When you contact us through our website form, our Facebook or Instagram lead forms, or by phone or email, we may collect your name, business name, phone number, email address, suburb, fleet and vehicle details, and any notes about the service you're after.

How we use it

We use your information solely to respond to your enquiry, provide a quote, book and deliver our services, and follow up with you. If you submit a Facebook or Instagram lead form, Meta passes your details to us in line with their terms so that we can get in touch.

Sharing your information

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information. We only share it with trusted providers that help us run the business — such as scheduling and communication tools — and only as much as they need to help us serve you.

Storage & security

Your information is stored securely, and access is limited to people who need it to do their job.

Marketing & opt-out

We may contact you about our services. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to a text message, unsubscribing from an email, or emailing us at info@primefleetcare.com.au.

Access, correction & deletion

You can ask us to access, correct or delete the personal information we hold about you at any time. Just get in touch.

Contact us

Questions about this policy or your information? Email info@primefleetcare.com.au or call 0449 162 476.
