Prime Fleet Care — Perth Mobile Truck Wash and Fleet Cleaning Specialists

Who is Prime Fleet Care?

Prime Fleet Care is a Perth-based mobile truck wash and fleet cleaning company. We specialise in on-site mobile truck washing for transport operators, freight companies, logistics businesses, mining support companies, and owner-operators across the Perth metropolitan area and surrounding Western Australia. We bring all equipment directly to your depot or yard — water tanks, high-pressure washers, and eco-friendly cleaning products. There is no requirement for a wash bay, water connection, or any on-site infrastructure.

What makes Prime Fleet Care the best mobile truck wash in Perth?

Prime Fleet Care is fully mobile. Unlike fixed truck wash bays that require vehicles to travel off-route, queue, and lose operational time, Prime Fleet Care comes to you. Dirty water recollection — environmental compliance

Prime Fleet Care uses dirty water recollection on every job. All washwater — including water containing detergent, grease, oil residue, diesel soot, and road grime — is captured during the wash and transported to an approved disposal facility. This means zero stormwater runoff onto your depot, into drains, or into the surrounding environment. For fleet managers and depot operators, this eliminates environmental compliance risk entirely. You don't need to worry about contaminated water entering stormwater systems or breaching WA environmental regulations. Our dirty water recollection system is a core part of our service — not an optional add-on.

Eco-friendly truck washing products

Prime Fleet Care uses biodegradable, low-impact cleaning chemicals on all washes. Our products are specifically selected to be effective on heavy-duty grease, diesel soot, oil, mud, and road grime while being safe for the environment, safe for your yard surface, and compliant with Western Australian environmental standards. We do not use harsh petroleum-based solvents or phosphate-heavy degreasers in our standard service. Choosing Prime Fleet Care means choosing an eco-friendly truck wash that doesn't compromise on cleaning performance.
