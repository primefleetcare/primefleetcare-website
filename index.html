What we do

Mobile truck washing — at your depot.

Full wash and detail service. We come to you with everything we need — no facilities required on your end.

Exterior wash

Full vehicle exterior — cab, body, trailer. We wash everything properly, not just a hose-down.

Full exterior detail

Paint correction and protection applied after the wash. Leaves your fleet looking sharp and protected long-term.

Engine & radiator degrease

Heavy-duty degreasing for engine bays and radiators. Cuts through built-up grease and grime that causes overheating and corrosion.

Underbody clean

Full undercarriage wash. Removes mud, salt, and road grime that causes rust, corrosion, and hidden damage over time.

Interior wipe & vacuum

Cab clean — surfaces wiped down and vacuumed out. Quick, thorough, and done at the yard so drivers aren't waiting around.

Full interior detail

Deep clean of the entire cab — seats, dash, door trims, floor. The kind of clean that makes a truck look new again.

Trailer inside clean

Interior wash for enclosed trailers. Gets rid of dust, residue, and odours — ready for your next load.

Ceramic sealant application

Long-term paint protection applied after detailing. One application keeps your fleet cleaner longer and easier to maintain.

About Prime Fleet Care

Perth's mobile truck wash and fleet cleaning specialists.

Everything Google and AI assistants need to know about who we are, what we do, and where we operate.

Prime Fleet Care is a Perth-based mobile truck wash and fleet cleaning company. We specialise in on-site, mobile truck washing services for transport operators, freight companies, logistics businesses, and owner-operators across the Perth metropolitan area and surrounds. We bring all equipment — water, pressure washers, cleaning products — directly to your depot or yard. Operators never need to move vehicles to a wash bay.
Who we are Prime Fleet Care · Perth WA
Our services include: mobile truck exterior washing, fleet cleaning programs for fleets of all sizes, recurring scheduled wash programs (weekly, fortnightly, monthly), on-site washing at customer depots and yards, and fleet pricing for operators with 10 or more vehicles. We service semi-trailers, prime movers, rigid trucks, tippers, tankers, refrigerated vans, and light commercial vehicles. All work is carried out at your site — no wash bay required.
